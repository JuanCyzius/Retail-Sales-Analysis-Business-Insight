# 🛒 Retail Sales Analysis — Business Insights

End-to-end data analysis project exploring retail sales performance using **SQL, Google Sheets, and Power BI** to generate actionable business insights.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a retail dataset to identify key business insights, revenue trends, product performance, and customer purchasing behavior.

The objective is to simulate the role of a **Data Analyst** working for a retail company, providing **actionable insights and strategic recommendations** to improve business performance.

The analysis combines:
- **SQL** → data exploration & aggregation  
- **Power BI** → data visualization & dashboards  

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The company wants to better understand:

- 📈 Revenue trends over time  
- 🛍️ Top-performing products  
- 🏬 Sales distribution across departments and sections  
- 👥 Customer purchasing behavior  
- ⚠️ Opportunities to increase revenue and reduce risk  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- SQL (SQLite)  
- Power BI  
- Google Sheets  
- Data Cleaning  
- Data Analysis  
- Data Visualization  
- Business Intelligence  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

- **Total Revenue:** €39,854,875  
- **Total Orders:** 2,060,188  
- **Average Order Value:** €19.34  
- **Average Revenue per Customer:** €219.09  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1️⃣ Significant Decline in Monthly Revenue
Revenue shows a consistent downward trend throughout the year, decreasing by **82% from January to December**.

**Possible causes:**
- Declining demand  
- Lack of promotional strategies  
- Seasonal effects  

---

### 2️⃣ High Revenue Concentration
Revenue is highly concentrated in a single department:

- **Department 4 → 93% of total revenue**

⚠️ This represents a major business risk due to overdependence.

---

### 3️⃣ Sales Concentrated in Few Sections
Three sections dominate most of the revenue:

- Section 24  
- Section 123  
- Section 83  

➡️ Together they generate **over 90% of total sales**

📌 Indicates low diversification.

---

### 4️⃣ Strong Dependence on Organic Products
Top-selling products are mostly organic:

- Bananas  
- Organic Bananas  
- Organic Strawberries  
- Organic Spinach  
- Organic Avocados  

📊 Could be:
- A competitive advantage  
- OR a vulnerability if trends change  

---

### 5️⃣ Customer Behavior

- Large customer base  
- Low-value purchases  
- No single customer dominates revenue  
- Small but frequent orders  

💡 Opportunity:
- Upselling  
- Cross-selling strategies  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 📌 Diversify Revenue Sources
Reduce dependency on a single department and limited sections.

**Impact:**
- Lower risk  
- More stable revenue  

---

### 📌 Expand Product Offering
Introduce non-organic and complementary products.

**Impact:**
- Broader market reach  
- Adaptation to changing preferences  

---

### 📌 Increase Average Order Value
Implement:
- Recommendation systems  
- Product bundles  

**Impact:**
- Higher revenue per transaction
